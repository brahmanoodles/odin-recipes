Carrot Apple Pancakes

Home

Description

These wholesome pancakes are packed with grated carrots and apples, making breakfast both delicious and nutritious.

A great way to sneak vegetables into your morning meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1 cup grated apple
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
  2. In another bowl, beat together milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Fold in grated carrots and apples.
  5. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium heat and grease lightly.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto the hot griddle.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on surface, about 2-3 minutes.
  8. Flip and cook until golden brown on the other side, about 2 minutes more.
  9. Serve warm with maple syrup or honey.
