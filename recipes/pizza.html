Pear and Gorgonzola Cheese Pizza

Home

Description

This gourmet pizza combines sweet pears with tangy Gorgonzola cheese for an elegant and unexpected flavor combination.

Perfect for impressing dinner guests or treating yourself to something special.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust or dough
  • 2 ripe pears, thinly sliced
  • 4 ounces Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Fresh arugula (optional)
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Brush crust with olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the crust.
  5. Arrange pear slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Scatter Gorgonzola cheese over the pears.
  7. Drizzle with honey and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Optional: Top with fresh arugula and balsamic glaze after baking.
  10. Slice and serve immediately.
